Service

  My previous summers were spent teaching kids in Houston how to play tennis. I love playing tennis, and to be able to spark that same passion in my neighborhood was incredibly fun and rewarding.

  Every week we got to see both returning and new campers ready to learn and improve their game, nd at the end of every week we would break off into a team competition to show what we had learned.
